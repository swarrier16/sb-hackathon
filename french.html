Conversational Phrases

Hello! My name is _____.
Bonjour! Je m'appelle ___________.(Insert name here)



How are you?
Comment ca va?
Answers: Ca va bien (I am good/well) or Ca va(I am okay) or Ca va mal(I am bad)



What is your name?
Comment t'appelle tu?
Answer: Je m'appelle ________(name)


Where do you live?
Ou est-ce que tu habite? Answer: J'habite a ...(City, or town)


What is your favorite food?
Quelle alimentaire a ton favorite?
Answer: Mon alimantaire favorite a ...(favorite food)


What is your favorite color?
Quelle a ton couleur favorite?
Answer: Mon couleur favorite a ...(favorite color)
